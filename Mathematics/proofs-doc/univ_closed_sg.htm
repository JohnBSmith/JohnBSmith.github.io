Theorem. univ_closed_sg
univ U → x ∈ U → {x} ∈ U
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ univ U, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ x ∈ U, hypo.
03. 1, 2 ⊢ {x, x} ∈ U, univ_closed_pair_set 1 2 2.
04. ⊢ {x, x} = {x}, union_idem.
05. 1, 2 ⊢ {x} ∈ U, eq_subst 4 3, P u ↔ u ∈ U.
univ_closed_sg. ⊢ univ U → x ∈ U → {x} ∈ U, subj_intro_ii 5.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on four axioms:
comp, eq_refl, eq_subst, ext.
